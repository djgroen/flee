# AGU PowerPoint Template Specifications

## Slide Setup

### Slide Size
- **Width**: 10 inches (or 1920 pixels)
- **Height**: 5.625 inches (or 1080 pixels)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **In PowerPoint**: Design → Slide Size → Widescreen (16:9)

### Safe Area
- **Top margin**: 0.5 inches (for title)
- **Bottom margin**: 0.5 inches (for footer/page number)
- **Side margins**: 0.5 inches each
- **Content area**: 9 × 4.625 inches

## Font Specifications

### Title Font
- **Font**: Arial or Calibri
- **Size**: 36-44pt
- **Weight**: Bold
- **Color**: Dark blue (#1f4788) or black

### Subtitle/Author
- **Font**: Arial or Calibri
- **Size**: 24-28pt
- **Weight**: Regular
- **Color**: Dark gray (#404040)

### Body Text
- **Font**: Arial or Calibri
- **Size**: 18-24pt (minimum 18pt for readability)
- **Weight**: Regular
- **Color**: Black or dark gray

### Bullet Points
- **Font**: Arial or Calibri
- **Size**: 18-20pt
- **Indentation**: 0.5 inches
- **Line spacing**: 1.2-1.5

### Figure Captions
- **Font**: Arial
- **Size**: 14-16pt
- **Weight**: Regular
- **Color**: Dark gray

## Color Scheme

### Primary Colors
- **Background**: White (#FFFFFF) or very light blue (#F0F8FF)
- **Text**: Black (#000000) or dark blue (#1f4788)
- **Accent**: Blue (#3498db) - matches current Beamer theme
- **Alert/Highlight**: Red (#e74c3c) for important points

### Color Palette
```
Background:     #FFFFFF (white)
Primary Text:   #000000 (black)
Secondary Text: #404040 (dark gray)
Accent Blue:    #3498db (current theme)
Dark Blue:      #1f4788 (AGU standard)
Alert Red:      #e74c3c (highlights)
```

## Master Slide Setup

### Title Master
- Title placeholder: Top center, 44pt, Bold
- Subtitle placeholder: Below title, 28pt
- Author placeholder: Bottom left, 24pt
- Date placeholder: Bottom right, 18pt

### Content Master
- Title placeholder: Top left, 32pt, Bold
- Content placeholder: Below title, 18-20pt
- Footer: Bottom center, 14pt (page number or conference name)

## Animation Guidelines

### Entry Animations
- **Title**: Fade In (0.5s)
- **Bullets**: Appear (one at a time, 0.3s each)
- **Figures**: Fade In (0.5s)

### Transitions
- **Between slides**: Fade (0.5s) or None
- **Avoid**: Complex transitions (distracting)

### Timing
- **Auto-advance**: Disabled (manual control)
- **Animation speed**: Medium (0.3-0.5s)

## Figure Guidelines

### Resolution
- **Minimum**: 300 DPI
- **Format**: PNG (preferred) or JPG
- **Size**: Embed, don't link (for portability)

### Placement
- **Full slide**: 90% of slide width, centered
- **Side-by-side**: 45% each, with gap
- **With text**: 50% width, text wraps or columns

### Captions
- **Format**: "Figure X: Description" (14-16pt)
- **Position**: Below figure, centered
- **Style**: Italic or regular (consistent)

## Math Equations

### Options
1. **PowerPoint Equation Editor** (Insert → Equation)
   - Type LaTeX-style: `P_{S2} = \Psi \times \Omega`
   - PowerPoint converts automatically

2. **MathType** (if available)
   - Better LaTeX compatibility
   - Professional formatting

3. **Screenshot from PDF**
   - Export equations as high-res images
   - Insert as pictures
   - Ensure 300 DPI

### Equation Formatting
- **Size**: 18-24pt (readable from back of room)
- **Color**: Black or dark blue
- **Background**: White or transparent
- **Spacing**: Adequate padding around equations

## Checklist for Each Slide

- [ ] Title is clear and descriptive
- [ ] Text is minimum 18pt
- [ ] Figures are high resolution (300 DPI)
- [ ] Colors have sufficient contrast
- [ ] Math equations are readable
- [ ] Animations are simple and professional
- [ ] Content fits within safe margins
- [ ] No overlapping elements
- [ ] Consistent font usage
- [ ] Proper alignment

## AGU-Specific Requirements

### File Naming
- Format: `AGU2024_LastName_FirstInitial.pptx`
- Example: `AGU2024_Puma_M.pptx`

### Submission
- **Format**: .pptx (PowerPoint 2016 or later)
- **Size**: Maximum 100 MB
- **Backup**: Also submit PDF version

### Presentation Setup
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Resolution**: 1920×1080 recommended
- **Test**: On Windows machine before presentation
- **Backup**: Bring PDF version on USB
