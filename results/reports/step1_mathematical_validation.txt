🧠 5-PARAMETER S1/S2 DUAL-PROCESS MODEL DEMONSTRATION
======================================================================

Mathematical Model:
  Ψᵢ = σ(α · education)
  Ωᵢ(t) = σ(β · (1 - conflict))
  P(S2) = Ψᵢ × Ωᵢ(t) × σ_η(conflict - θ)
  p_move = (1 - P(S2)) · movechance + P(S2) · p_S2

Parameters:
  α = 2.0  (Education sensitivity)
  β = 2.0  (Conflict sensitivity)
  η = 4.0  (S2 activation steepness)
  θ = 0.5  (S2 threshold)
  p_S2 = 0.8  (S2 move probability)
