# Roadmap

This page tracks planned improvements and known limitations of Flee.

!!! note
    This page is a placeholder. Specific roadmap items will be added here over time. For the most up-to-date information on planned features, see the [GitHub issues](https://github.com/djgroen/flee/issues).

---

## Known limitations

- Flood-driven and conflict-driven spawning cannot be combined in a single simulation
- `flood_link_weights` is not yet supported in DFlee
- The Flare conflict evolution model is still in prototype stage

---

## Planned improvements

- Combined disaster + conflict scenarios
- Improved validation metrics and reporting
- Extended demographic modelling
