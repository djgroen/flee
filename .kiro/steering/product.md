# Product Overview

Flee is an agent-based modeling toolkit purpose-built for simulating the movement of individuals across geographical locations. It is primarily used for modeling the movements of forcibly displaced persons (FDPs) such as refugees and internally displaced people.

## Key Features

- Agent-based simulation of human displacement and migration
- Support for conflict-driven and disaster-driven displacement scenarios
- Parallel processing capabilities using MPI
- Multiscale simulation support for coupling macro and micro models
- Validation against real-world UNHCR refugee data
- Ensemble simulation capabilities for uncertainty quantification

## Primary Use Cases

- Refugee movement prediction and analysis
- Disaster-driven displacement modeling (floods, conflicts)
- Policy impact assessment for humanitarian response
- Academic research in computational social science
- Validation studies comparing simulated vs. actual displacement patterns

## Target Users

- Humanitarian organizations and NGOs
- Academic researchers in migration studies
- Policy makers and government agencies
- Data scientists working on social simulation
