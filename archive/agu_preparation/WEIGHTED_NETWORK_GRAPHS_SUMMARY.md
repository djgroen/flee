1. **🎯 Clear Node Positioning**
   - **Red nodes** = Origin/Conflict zones (where agents start)
   - **Orange nodes** = Transit towns (intermediate stops)
   - **Green nodes** = Refugee camps (final destinations)

   - **Only significant flows** (>5 agents) are labeled

4. **📋 Flow Statistics**
